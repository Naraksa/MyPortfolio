My Vision

My journey begins with volunteering. Through participating in various programs such as fellowships and student ambassador roles, I aim to enhance my soft skills—particularly communication, leadership, and teamwork—while also broadening my personal and professional network.

Alongside these efforts, I plan to pursue a work-study lifestyle to help support my daily expenses and build independence. As I grow both academically and personally, I will seek out opportunities for exchange programs that allow me to travel across different countries, learn about diverse cultures, and take time to relax and recharge.

After completing my undergraduate degree, I intend to pursue a master’s degree abroad—continuing to expand my global perspective and deepen my expertise. Eventually, with the knowledge, experience, and values I have gained, I aspire to establish my own business and create something meaningful that reflects my journey and purpose.
